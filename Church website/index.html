About the Church

We engage in a variety of spiritual activities to grow believers and advance God’s kingdom: discipleship, prophecy training, worship, teaching, spiritual warfare, scientific research, and community outreach. Our programs are designed to develop mature followers of Christ who can impact families, society, and nations.

The church is structured into four chambers, each with a unique focus:

  • The House of Philip (THoP): Training daughters in prophecy, purity, leadership, and family stewardship.
  • The Harpers: Equipping musicians to lead worship and proclaim God’s glory through song.
  • The Treasure House: Raising speakers and writers to understand and declare the mysteries of God.
  • The Apothecary: Developing prophetic scientists who discern spiritual realities and bring solutions to physical and spiritual needs.

Mission: To raise and equip end-time laborers—Intercessors, Evangelists, and Shepherds—to reap and preserve the harvest and to herald the glory of the Lord.

Vision: Preparing the way of the Lord.
